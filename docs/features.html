Features

Load the followers of the current page as well as the people it follows.

Load the people not following back.

Load the people who have unfollowed since the last check.

Global users queue search by username and full name.

Show who has viewed any of your last active stories.

Remove specific people from the queue.

Save and load queues in order to be processed later.

Settings allowing you to configure different parts of the extension.

Follow / Unfollow modes for processing the users queue.
